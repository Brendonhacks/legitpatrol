Our Story

Legit Patrol was founded in 2017 with a powerful mission: to bridge the gap between theft and recovery. What started as a small team of three passionate individuals has grown into a full-scale operation with dozens of specialists across vehicle tracking and pet search operations.

Our founder, Alexander Reid, experienced the frustration of having a vehicle stolen with no actionable help — and later, the heartbreak of a missing family dog. Those experiences became the catalyst for building a company that combines cutting-edge tracking technology with relentless determination.

Today, we serve clients worldwide, recovering thousands of vehicles and reuniting hundreds of families with their beloved pets every year. Our reputation is built on results, not promises.

LEADERSHIP

Meet Our Founder

FOUNDER & CEO

Alexander Reid

Chief Executive Officer

With over 15 years of experience in private investigations and asset recovery, Alexander founded Legit Patrol after personally experiencing the helplessness of vehicle theft and the heartbreak of a missing family pet. His vision was clear: build a team that actually gets results.

Under his leadership, Legit Patrol has grown from a three-person operation to a nationwide service with a 99% success rate across vehicle and pet recoveries. Alexander holds certifications in GPS tracking systems, drone operations, and advanced surveillance techniques.

He is a frequent speaker at security conferences and a passionate advocate for animal welfare and theft prevention.

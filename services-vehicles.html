VEHICLE FAQ

Frequently Asked Questions

How quickly can you start tracking my stolen vehicle?

We begin tracking within 1 hour of receiving your case. Our team mobilizes field agents and activates GPS/ANPR systems immediately after case assessment and verification.

Do I need a police report before contacting you?

While a police report is helpful, you can contact us immediately after the theft. We can begin tracking while you file the report.

What if my vehicle doesn't have a GPS tracker installed?

No problem. We use ANPR cameras, satellite imaging, OSINT intelligence, and ground teams to locate vehicles even without built-in trackers.

What information do you need from me?

Vehicle registration number, VIN/chassis number, make/model/color, last known location, date and time of theft, and any relevant photos or CCTV footage.

How much does vehicle recovery cost?

Pricing depends on vehicle type and complexity. We provide a transparent quote upfront — with no hidden fees.

Can you recover vehicles taken abroad?

Yes, we have cross-border coordination capabilities and international recovery partnerships.

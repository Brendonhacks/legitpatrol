OUR METHODS

How We Search for Pets

Drone Surveillance

Thermal imaging drones scan large areas from above, detecting animal heat signatures even in dense vegetation or at night.

Microchip Scanning

We scan all local vets, shelters, and rescue centers while monitoring national microchip databases in real-time.

Community Alert Networks

Instant social media alert blasts to thousands of local residents, pet groups, and community watch networks.

Scent Trailing

For dogs, we deploy trained scent-trailing dogs that follow your pet's unique scent trail from the last known location.

Frequently Asked Questions

How quickly can you start searching for my pet?

We launch community alerts within 30 minutes. Physical search teams and drone deployment begin within 2-4 hours.

Dogs, cats, birds, rabbits, guinea pigs, ferrets, hamsters, horses, donkeys, reptiles, and most exotic animals.

How much does pet recovery cost?

Pricing depends on pet type and search area. We provide a transparent quote before starting with no hidden fees.
